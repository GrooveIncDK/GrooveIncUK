About our company

Providing development of new agricultural inputs and systems!

We are a world-leading provider of beef and agricultural products. We own and operate a strategic balance of properties, feedlots and farms comprising around 10 million hectares of land. This equates to roughly 1% of country's land mass. Having been established in 1826, we are the oldest continuously operating company in the country.

We are committed to developing our operating systems using the world's best practices and have company-wide accreditation through different quality assurance programs. With an established track record of environmentally sensitive land use, we are one of the first companies to employ our own field rangelands officer.
